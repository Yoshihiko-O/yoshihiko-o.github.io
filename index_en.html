Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is Eyesight Recovery 3D Training?

A: It is a vision training app using 3D stereogram technology. Just a few minutes of daily training helps strengthen eye muscles and reduce eye strain.

Q: How do stereograms work?

A: Stereograms are a visual training method that trains the eye's focusing function (ciliary muscles) by viewing special pattern images. By using parallel or cross-eyed viewing methods, a hidden 3D image emerges.

Q: How often should I train?

A: We recommend training for about 3-5 minutes per day. Consistent practice is key to seeing results. If you feel any eye strain, please take a break.

Q: What is a Gabor Patch?

A: Gabor patches are one of the scientifically proven visual training methods that use striped patterns. They are known to improve the brain's visual processing capabilities.

Q: What is Creative Mode?

A: Creative Mode automatically generates various patterns such as emojis, mathematical formulas, and Gabor patches. This keeps training fresh and engaging every time.

Q: Can children use this app?

A: Yes, children can safely use this app. However, if they experience any eye discomfort, please stop using it and consult an eye doctor.

Q: Is the app free to use?

A: Basic features are available for free. You can download it from the App Store and start training immediately.

Q: Does it work on both iPhone and iPad?

A: Yes, the app is compatible with both iPhone and iPad. Enjoy training with an optimized display on either device.
